What is Loan EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment that a borrower needs to make to repay their loan. The EMI amount depends on the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

How is EMI Calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1]

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12 / 100)
  • N = Loan Tenure in Months
