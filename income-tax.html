How to Use the Advanced Income Tax Calculator?

Use this tool to accurately calculate your tax liability, including deductions and exemptions.

Steps:

  • Enter your annual income.
  • Enter your eligible deductions (e.g., investments, insurance).
  • Enter applicable exemptions (e.g., HRA, medical allowance).
  • Select your tax slab.
  • Click "Calculate" to see your net taxable income and final tax amount.
