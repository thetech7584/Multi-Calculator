Maintaining a healthy body fat percentage is essential for overall fitness and well-being. A body fat calculator helps you track your progress and understand how diet and exercise affect your body composition.

Why Measure Body Fat?

  • Helps track weight loss and muscle gain
  • Determines risk for obesity-related diseases
  • Provides better insights than BMI alone

How to Use a Body Fat Calculator?

To estimate your body fat, enter your weight, waist, neck, and optionally hip measurements. The calculator uses these values to estimate fat percentage using a scientifically validated formula.

What is a Healthy Body Fat Percentage?

  • Men: 6-24% (Athletes: 6-13%)
  • Women: 16-30% (Athletes: 14-20%)

Tips to Reduce Body Fat

  • Follow a balanced diet with protein, fiber, and healthy fats
  • Engage in regular strength training and cardio
  • Stay hydrated and maintain good sleep patterns
