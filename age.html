about Age Calculator.

Age Calculator calculates your Dob (Date of birth) with current date and tells your actual age in years, months, and days. This calculator gives accurate result based on your date of birth..

How to Calculate Age?

The age is calculated by subtracting the birth date from the current date. The result gives the number of years, months, and days.

Why Use an Age Calculator?

  • Quick and accurate age calculation.
  • Useful for official documentation.
  • Find out how many days until your next birthday.
