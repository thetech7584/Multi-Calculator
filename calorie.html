How Are Daily Calories Calculated?

The calculator uses the BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) formula, which determines how many calories your body burns at rest, and then multiplies it by your activity level.

Why Should You Track Calories?

  • Helps with weight loss, gain, or maintenance
  • Ensures proper nutrition for better energy and health
  • Prevents overeating or undereating

How to Use the Calculator?
